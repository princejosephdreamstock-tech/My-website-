This Agreement is entered into between Axiom Forensics ("the Firm") and the organisation identified in the accompanying Evidence Request confirmation ("the Client"). Clause 02
Scope of the Meridian Forensic Audit

The Meridian Forensic Audit is a Technical Forensic Assessment of the Client's AI systems as identified and submitted via the Evidence Request List (AFP-ERL-001). The assessment covers the following forensic pillars:

Pillar I — Intervention Architecture: Assessment of the human override and intervention infrastructure against Article 14(1) and Article 14(3) requirements.

Pillar II — Log Integrity and Traceability: Examination of AI decision log architecture, shared event identifiers, and audit trail completeness against Article 12(1) requirements.

Pillar III — Comprehensibility Assurance: Assessment of the AI system's explainability infrastructure and its capacity to surface decision rationale to designated oversight personnel.

Pillar IV — Scope and Role Assignment: Review of Article 14(4) designation records, named oversight authority documentation, and role-based access controls.

Pillar V — Feedback Loop Integration: Assessment of post-market monitoring architecture and the integration of reviewer feedback into model governance.

The Firm will deliver a Meridian Report containing: an Axiom Oversight Metric (AOM) score, critical findings with article-specific references, a risk band classification, and a 30/60/90-day remediation roadmap.

Clause 03
Zero-Call Engagement Protocol
AFP-AGR-001 · Clause 3.1 · Zero-Call Protocol

The Firm does not conduct discovery calls, scoping calls, introductory calls, video consultations, or any form of synchronous verbal engagement at any stage of this or any other engagement. This is a structural operating principle of the Firm, not a scheduling preference. The Client expressly acknowledges and accepts this condition as a prerequisite of engagement.

All clarification, scoping, and technical questions are to be submitted via the Secure Evidence Vault or by email to audit@axiomforensics.cloud. Written responses are issued within 4 business hours during the active engagement window. The Firm's written responses constitute the entirety of its advisory output for the purposes of this engagement.

Any Client request for a call, meeting, or video consultation will be declined. Such a request does not constitute grounds for a refund or engagement termination by the Client.

Clause 04
Nature of Service — Technical Assessment Not Statutory Audit
AFP-AGR-001 · Clause 4.1 · Limitation of Character

The Meridian Forensic Audit constitutes a Technical Forensic Assessment under Axiom Forensics Audit Standard AFP-002. It does not constitute, and must not be represented to any third party as constituting, a statutory audit, a legal audit, a compliance certification, a legal opinion, a regulatory clearance, or an assurance engagement within the meaning of any professional standards body.

The Meridian Report is a technical document identifying forensic indicators of compliance risk. It does not guarantee compliance with the EU AI Act or immunity from enforcement action by the Irish National AI Office, the Central Bank of Ireland, or any other national competent authority. The Client must seek independent legal advice before taking any regulatory, commercial, or operational action in reliance on this report.

Third-party reliance is expressly excluded. Axiom Forensics accepts no duty of care to any third party who may receive or rely upon the Meridian Report.
